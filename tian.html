The Master

大红袍艺术家 · People's Fine Arts Publishing House

Tian Gencheng stands among China's most honored living artists. His work has been selected for publication in the "Red Robe" (大红袍) series by the People's Fine Arts Publishing House — the same prestigious series that immortalized masters including Xu Beihong, Qi Baishi, Zhang Daqian, and Guan Shan Yue.

The "Red Robe" designation represents the highest recognition in Chinese art publishing. Each artist featured has shaped the course of Chinese art history. Master Tian's inclusion places him among these revered figures.

Beyond his own artistic mastery, Master Tian dedicates himself to nurturing the next generation of Chinese brush painters. His trained students carry forward his techniques, philosophy, and aesthetic vision — each piece they create bears the lineage of this distinguished master.

China's National Publisher

The People's Fine Arts Publishing House (人民美术出版社) is China's state-run art publisher. The Red Robe series is not commercial — it is a historical record of masters who shaped Chinese art.

🏆

Among the Immortals

Past honorees read like a who's who: Xu Beihong, Qi Baishi, Zhang Daqian, Guan Shan Yue, Huang Binhong, Fu Baoshi, Li Keran. Master Tian joins this legendary company.

✍️

Living Tradition

While most Red Robe artists are historical, Master Tian represents a living legacy. His trained students carry forward techniques that define this prestigious lineage.
